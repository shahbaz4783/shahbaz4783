Artificial Intelligence

Intro to AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on creating intelligent machines that can think and act like humans. It has the potential to revolutionize many industries by automating tasks and making them more efficient.

AI in Healthcare

AI is being used in healthcare to analyze medical images, identify patterns in patient data, and assist with tasks like scheduling appointments and managing electronic medical records.

AI Ethics

As AI becomes more prevalent in society, it's important to consider the ethical implications of this technology. Key ethical concerns include issues of bias, privacy, and accountability.
